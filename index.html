Product promise

A reader designed around ownership, not lock-in.

MIYO keeps the public website honest: real screenshots, direct release links, clear source boundaries, and no hosted manga or third-party catalogs.

01 · Reader

Offline-first chapter loading

Downloaded pages are treated as primary content, so local chapters remain the center of the reading experience when network providers are unreliable.

02 · Sources

Parser boundaries stay clear

Source logic is kept separate from the reader where possible through parser infrastructure and guarded plugin imports.

03 · Releases

Optional assets stay visible

APK and refinement model packages are release assets, not invisible bloat. Users can inspect what they install.
